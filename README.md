## 🎯 What is ClauseNine?

ClauseNine monitors SEBI, RBI, GST, and BMC regulations and alerts you only about rules that actually affect your specific business or investment portfolio.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Personalized Compliance Profiling** - Understands your business/portfolio
- **Automated Regulation Scanning** - Monitors 4 regulatory bodies every 6 hours
- **Smart Pre-filtering** - Eliminates 60-80% irrelevant regulations
- **AI Impact Analysis** - Explains specific impact with actionable recommendations
- **Adaptive Learning** - Improves accuracy based on your feedback

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, Vercel
- **Backend**: FastAPI, Supabase
- **AI**: Gemini/GPT APIs
- **Tools**: Redis, n8n, Playwright
